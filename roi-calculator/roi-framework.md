# ROI Calculation Framework

Use this to build the business case for any AWS project.

---

## The ROI Formula

ROI = (Benefits - Costs) / Costs × 100

A 500% ROI means for every $1 spent you get $5 back.

## Presenting ROI To Non-Technical Stakeholders

**Don't say:** "We'll implement a serverless microservices architecture with DynamoDB and Lambda"

**Do say:** "We'll automate your manual process, saving your team 20 hours per week — that's $52,000 per year in recovered time, for an infrastructure cost of $200/month."

Always translate technology into **time saved**, **money saved**, or **revenue gained**.
